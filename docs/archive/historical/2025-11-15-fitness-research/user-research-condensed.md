## ðŸ§  Behavioral Psychology Insights

### **What Drives Retention (2025 Research):**

1. **Intrinsic Motivation > Extrinsic:**
   - âœ… **Challenge** (progressive overload, PRs) - Strongest predictor of continued use
   - âœ… **Curiosity** (experiment with programs, try new exercises)
   - âœ… **Competence** (see measurable progress, master techniques)
   - âš ï¸ **Autonomy** (user control over goals) - Apps that force goals â†’ churn
   - âš ï¸ **Relatedness** (social connection) - Most apps lack this

2. **Habit Formation Mechanics:**
   - **Streaks** (Duolingo-style) - 56% of users access apps 10+ times/week when streaks present
   - **Daily rituals** (morning check-in) - Creates routine anchoring
   - **Micro-rewards** (badges at 7, 30, 100 days) - Dopamine hits sustain motivation
   - **Social accountability** (mikroklub) - 3x higher retention vs solo users

3. **What KILLS Motivation:**
   - âŒ **Unattainable goals** (MyFitnessPal calorie warnings â†’ shame â†’ abandonment)
   - âŒ **Obsession with numbers** (users pacing rooms at midnight to close Apple Watch rings)
   - âŒ **Guilt-based notifications** ("You missed your workout!" â†’ defensive reaction â†’ disable notifications)
   - âŒ **Complexity** (60+ inputs per workout â†’ cognitive overload â†’ abandonment)

### **GymApp Psychological Design Principles:**

1. **Autonomy-Supportive:**
   - Let users choose workout intensity (don't force "burn 500 calories!")
   - Offer rest day option in daily check-in (don't guilt-trip)
   - Allow manual/voice/camera input choice (flexibility)

2. **Competence-Building:**
   - Show concrete progress ("You added +5kg to squat!")
   - Celebrate milestones (badges, animations, confetti)
   - Weekly reports with NUMBERS (not "great week!")

3. **Relatedness-Fostering:**
   - Mikroklub (intimate 10-person groups vs anonymous leaderboards)
   - Tandem training (workout WITH friend, not just share after)
   - WhatsApp-style invites (connect with existing social network)

4. **Entertainment-Driven:**
   - AI personality options (Zen, Motivator, Drill Sergeant)
   - "Living App" concept (app responds to YOUR state, not generic)
   - Visual progress (RPG body zones, level-up animations)

### **Interview Questions (30 min, semi-structured):**

6. If you could change ONE thing about fitness apps, what would it be?

8. How do you measure progress? (Probe: weight, strength, aesthetics, feel)

**Section 3: Feature Validation (10 min)**
9. **Show mockup:** "This app remembers your last workout and pre-fills the form. How valuable is this?" (1-10 scale)
10. **Show streak system:** "You earn badges at 7, 30, 100 days. Would this motivate you?" (Yes/No, probe why)
11. **Show mikroklub concept:** "10-person, 6-week challenges with people you choose. Thoughts?" (Probe: prefer this vs leaderboard?)
12. **Show AI mood adaptation:** "App adjusts workout based on sleep/stress. Creepy or helpful?" (Probe: privacy concerns)

**Section 4: Willingness to Pay (5 min)**
13. What's the most you've paid for a fitness app subscription? (Probe: monthly vs annual)
14. If GymApp costs Â£2.99/month with [list MVP features], would you subscribe? (Yes/Probably/No)
15. What features would make you DEFINITELY subscribe? (Rank top 3)
