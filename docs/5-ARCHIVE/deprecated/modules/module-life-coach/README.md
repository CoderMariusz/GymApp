## üìã Module Overview

**What is Life Coach AI?**

The central AI-powered brain of the LifeCoach AI ecosystem. Analyzes your entire life (sleep, activity, mood, goals) and generates personalized daily plans.

**Key Features (Planned):**
- Daily "life plan" generated by AI
- Analysis of sleep, activity, mood, goals
- Proactive recommendations:
  - Suggested workouts
  - Meditation sessions
  - Social tasks
  - Learning activities
  - Development challenges
- Motivational conversations with AI coach
- Emotional intelligence (adapts to your mood)
- Goal tracking and habit formation

**Target Users:**
- People seeking structure and guidance
- Goal-oriented individuals
- Users overwhelmed by daily decisions
- Anyone wanting to optimize their life

## üèóÔ∏è Planned Architecture

### AI Integration

**Core AI Features:**
1. **Daily Plan Generation:**
   - Analyzes user data (sleep, activity, mood, goals)
   - Generates prioritized task list
   - Balances different life areas
   - Adapts to available time and energy

2. **Conversational Coaching:**
   - Chat interface with AI coach
   - Motivational conversations
   - Goal-setting dialogues
   - Reflection prompts

3. **Emotional Intelligence:**
   - Detects user mood from inputs
   - Adjusts communication style
   - Provides appropriate support
   - Suggests mood-boosting activities

4. **Habit Formation:**
   - Tracks streaks and patterns
   - Reinforces positive behaviors
   - Identifies obstacles
   - Suggests interventions

### Data Sources

**From Other Modules:**
- **Fitness:** Workout frequency, energy levels, rest days
- **Mind & Emotion:** Stress levels, meditation practice, mood
- **Talent Tree:** Current XP, unlocked abilities, active challenges
- **Relationship:** Social activity, communication patterns
- **Learning:** Course progress, learning time

**User Inputs:**
- Daily check-ins (mood, energy, sleep quality)
- Goals and priorities
- Available time
- Preferences and constraints

### Technology Stack

**AI Services (To Be Decided):**
- Option 1: OpenAI GPT-4 (best quality, highest cost)
- Option 2: Anthropic Claude (great for conversations)
- Option 3: Open-source LLMs (Llama, cost-effective)
- Option 4: Hybrid (GPT-4 for premium, open-source for free)

**Backend:**
- Shared with ecosystem (Firebase/Supabase)
- Real-time data sync from modules
- User preference storage
- Conversation history

**Frontend:**
- Flutter (shared with ecosystem)
- Chat UI for AI conversations
- Daily plan dashboard
- Goal tracking widgets

## üìù Notes

**Why This is the Core Module:**
- Other modules are specialists (fitness, mental health, etc.)
- Life Coach AI is the generalist that connects everything
- Without Life Coach, modules work independently
- With Life Coach, modules create a cohesive life improvement system

**Relationship to Other Modules:**
- **Standalone:** Life Coach can work alone (basic life planning)
- **Enhanced:** Gets better with more modules (more data = better plans)
- **Required:** Not mandatory for other modules to work

**Free vs Premium:**
- Free: Basic daily planning, limited AI conversations
- Premium: Unlimited AI conversations, advanced planning, predictive insights
