## üí∏ Monetization Strategy (Based on 2025 Benchmarks)

### Primary Model: **Hybrid Freemium**

**Free Tier (60% retention goal):**
- Manual workout logging with smart pattern memory
- Basic progress tracking
- Streak system & daily check-ins
- Exercise library (limited)

**Premium Tier ($2.99/month or $29.99/year):**
- AI workout suggestions
- Advanced analytics & reports
- Unlimited exercise library
- Integration with diet apps (Fitatu, MyFitnessPal)
- Priority support

**In-App Purchases (IAP):**
- Specialized workout programs ($4.99 - $9.99)
- Nutrition plans ($9.99)
- One-time features (custom templates: $1.99)

**Future Revenue Streams (P2/P3):**
- Affiliate partnerships (supplements, wearables: 5-15% commission)
- Trainer marketplace (20% platform fee)
- B2B gym partnerships (white-label licensing)

### Pricing Psychology (2025 Best Practices)
- Weekly trial ($0.99 for 7 days) ‚Üí Monthly conversion
- Annual discount (save 16%: $29.99 vs $35.88)
- Outcome-based messaging: "Unlock your personalized AI coach" vs "Premium features"

## üìâ Key Performance Benchmarks

### User Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- **Industry Average:** $20 - $30 per user
- **GymApp Target:** $15 - $25 (leveraging organic/viral growth)
- **Channels:** Social media ($10-15), App Store Optimization (free), Gym partnerships ($5-10)

### Retention Rates
- **Industry Average (Day 30):** 3% - 3.7%
- **Top Performers:** 20% - 31% (CashWalk, Sweatcoin)

- **Subscription Retention (Annual):** 33%

### Churn Rate
- **Industry Average:** 12%
- **Best-in-class:** 7% (Sworkit)

### Critical Success Metrics
- **Day 3 Retention:** >23% (industry loses 77% by day 3)
